Claude Subscription Gateway "{{ .Release.Name }}" is deploying.

1) Log in to your Claude subscription ONCE (creds persist on the PVC across
   restarts — no need to repeat):

     kubectl exec -it deploy/{{ include "claude-sub-gateway.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -- claude

   Choose "Log in with Claude subscription", open the printed URL, finish, quit.
   No pod restart is needed afterward.

{{- if .Values.ingress.enabled }}

{{- else }}
     kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "claude-sub-gateway.fullname" . }} 8000:{{ .Values.service.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
     curl -H "Authorization: Bearer <your-key>" http://localhost:8000/v1/models
